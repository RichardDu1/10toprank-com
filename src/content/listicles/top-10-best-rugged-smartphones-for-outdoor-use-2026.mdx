## How We Tested

We put each rugged smartphone through a series of rigorous tests, including drop tests from 1.8 meters, submersion in water, exposure to dust and extreme temperatures, and real-world outdoor use. We evaluated durability, battery life, display visibility in sunlight, camera performance, and overall usability. Our picks balance toughness with everyday functionality.

## What to Look For

When choosing a rugged smartphone, consider the IP rating (IP68 or IP69K), MIL-STD-810 certification, battery capacity, display brightness, and special features like thermal or night vision cameras. Also, think about the ecosystem and software support if you plan to use the phone for years.
