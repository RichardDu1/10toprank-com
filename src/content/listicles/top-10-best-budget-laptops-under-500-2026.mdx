## What to Look For

When shopping for a budget laptop under $500, prioritize a Full HD display (1920x1080), at least 8GB of RAM, and a solid-state drive (SSD) for fast boot times. Intel Core i3 or AMD Ryzen 3 processors are ideal. Battery life should be at least 6 hours. Consider your needs: Chromebooks are great for web-based tasks, while Windows laptops offer more software compatibility. 2-in-1s add versatility. Always check for sales and discounts to get the best deal.
