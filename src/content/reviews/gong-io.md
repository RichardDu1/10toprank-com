## Core Features

Gong.io captures and analyzes every customer interaction across phone, email, and video conferencing. Key features include:

- **Call Recording & Transcription:** Automatically records and transcribes calls with high accuracy.
- **AI-Powered Insights:** Identifies talk-to-listen ratios, keyword mentions, competitor references, and sentiment.
- **Deal Risk Scoring:** Flags deals at risk based on conversation patterns and engagement metrics.
- **Coaching & Training:** Provides managers with specific clips and metrics for coaching reps.
- **CRM Integration:** Syncs with Salesforce, HubSpot, and other CRMs to enrich deal records.

## Pricing Breakdown

Gong does not publicly disclose pricing. Based on user reports and industry analysis, pricing is typically:

- **Per-user, per-year:** $10,000–$15,000 per user annually.
- **Minimum commitment:** Often requires a minimum number of users (e.g., 10+).
- **Enterprise plan:** Custom pricing for large deployments, including dedicated support and advanced analytics.

There is no free tier or trial; demos are provided upon request.

## Ease of Use

Gong’s interface is modern and intuitive for basic tasks like reviewing call recordings and reading transcripts. However, the depth of analytics can be overwhelming for new users. The platform requires significant setup, including integration with telephony systems and CRM mapping. Training is essential to leverage advanced features like deal scoring and coaching dashboards.

## Integration Ecosystem

Gong integrates natively with:

- **CRMs:** Salesforce, HubSpot, Microsoft Dynamics, Zoho.
- **Conferencing:** Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Webex.
- **Dialers:** RingCentral, Five9, Aircall.
- **Email:** Outlook, Gmail.

It also offers a REST API for custom integrations.

## Customer Support

Gong provides 24/7 support via chat, email, and phone for enterprise customers. Onboarding includes dedicated success managers and training sessions. Community forums and knowledge base are available, but advanced support is gated behind premium tiers.

## Security & Compliance

Gong is SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit. However, recording all interactions raises privacy concerns; organizations must ensure compliance with local consent laws (e.g., two-party consent states in the US). Gong provides features to pause recording and redact sensitive information.

## Alternatives

- **Chorus.ai (ZoomInfo):** Similar AI conversation intelligence, often slightly cheaper.
- **Clari:** Focuses on revenue forecasting and deal intelligence.
- **Salesloft:** Combines conversation intelligence with sales engagement.
- **Jiminny:** Lower-cost alternative for SMBs.

## Final Verdict

Gong.io is a powerful tool for enterprises serious about data-driven sales optimization. Its AI insights are industry-leading, but the high cost and complexity limit its appeal. For smaller teams or those with tighter budgets, exploring alternatives like Chorus or Salesloft may provide better ROI.
